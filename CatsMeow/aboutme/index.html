About Me

Hey! I’m Kiana and I absolutely love being a waxer!!! I went to school to become an Aesthetician specifically to do waxing in 2010. Waxing definitely is my favorite thing to do. I offer full body speed waxing, specializing in Brazilians. All are welcome in this safe space! No body shaming or judgement. Waxing is a lifestyle and a personal journey. It is a great feeling seeing a client who is instantly happy from having their unwanted hair gone. Let me help you to be hair free in the style your prefer.

I am also a Certified Make Up Artist. My others services offered are treatments for Hyperpigmentation, Lash Tints & Lifts, Brow Tint/Stains. Who needs therapy when you can chat it up with me while having your personal grooming needs helped too? I try to make every appointment entertaining and enjoyable especially for waxing because having your hair ripped out is so much fun, so I'll crack jokes and keep you distracted. My room is located inside the Golden Hands Salon by appointment only. If you need a certain time not offered, e.g. 7am, please let me know and I’ll try to be as accommodating as possible to both our schedules.
I'm looking forward to meeting you!
